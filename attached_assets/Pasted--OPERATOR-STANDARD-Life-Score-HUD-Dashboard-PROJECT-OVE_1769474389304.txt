# OPERATOR STANDARD - Life Score HUD Dashboard

## PROJECT OVERVIEW

Build a new home screen dashboard for The Operator Standard app called the "Operator HUD." This replaces the current home screen with a behavioral identity system that shows users who they are becoming based on their actions - not just whether they completed tasks.

Think of it like a **Madden Ultimate Team player card** meets **Instagram Creator Dashboard**. Clean, serious, honest. Not gamified with confetti. The kind of screen you open and immediately know exactly where you stand.

---

## DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

**Core Principle:** This is not motivation. This is confrontation. The user cannot argue with these numbers. They cannot hide from them.

**Visual Style:**
- Clean, minimal, serious
- Dark mode primary (black/dark gray background)
- Accent color: Red (#E63946) for the Operator Standard brand
- Secondary: White text, gray for secondary elements
- No charts, no graphs, no walls of numbers
- Immediate comprehension, not analysis
- Mobile-first design (375px width base)

**Font Suggestions:**
- Headers: Bold, all-caps, condensed (similar to sports/military aesthetic)
- Numbers: Large, bold, impactful
- Body: Clean sans-serif

## RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR

**Mobile (375px - default):**
- Single column layout
- Life Score: 120px diameter circle
- Attributes: Full-width bars

**Tablet (768px+):**
- Life Score and Vital Stats could be side by side
- Attributes could be 2-column grid

**Desktop (1024px+):**
- Max-width container (480px) centered
- Maintains mobile-first aesthetic
- Optional: Attributes as a 2x4 grid

## ANIMATIONS & INTERACTIONS

1. **Life Score Ring:** Subtle pulsing glow animation (CSS keyframes)
2. **Attribute Bars:** Animate fill on load (width transition from 0 to score%)
3. **Score Changes:** Number counter animation when score updates
4. **Card Hover:** Subtle lift/shadow on hover (desktop)
5. **Pull to Refresh:** Could refresh all stats

---

## OPTIONAL ENHANCEMENTS (Phase 2)

1. **Time Range Toggle:** Switch between 7-day, 30-day, 90-day views
2. **Attribute Detail View:** Tap an attribute to see breakdown
3. **Historical Graph:** Simple trend line for Life Score over time
4. **Share Card:** Export your player card as an image
5. **Comparison:** See how you rank vs. other operators

---

## WHAT THIS SHOULD FEEL LIKE

When a user opens this screen, they should:

1. **Immediately know their Life Score** - The one number that matters
2. **See their weakest areas highlighted** - Can't hide from red attributes
3. **Read ARCANE's truth** - One clear statement about what's broken
4. **Feel like the system knows them** - Better than they know themselves
5. **Have no room for self-deception** - "I'm doing pretty good" is replaced by "My relationships are at 29"

---

## IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

- This is a **home screen replacement**, not an addition
- Keep existing navigation and header patterns
- The Life Score should be calculated server-side (for now, use mock data)
- Attributes are NOT user-customizable - they're fixed categories
- Attributes are NOT self-reported - they're calculated from behavior
- The ARCANE directive should feel AI-generated and personal
