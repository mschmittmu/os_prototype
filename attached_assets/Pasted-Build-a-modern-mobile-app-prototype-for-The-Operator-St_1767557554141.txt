Build a modern mobile app prototype for "The Operator Standard" - a discipline and accountability lifestyle app. This should feel like the command center for people taking control of their lives. Think: the intensity of a military ops dashboard meets the polish of Strava meets the community of Discord.
BRAND ESSENCE

Philosophy: "The Operator Sets The Standard. The AI Enforces It."
Core Values: Discipline Over Emotion, Extreme Accountability, Truth First Always, Physical Excellence, Mission Over Self, Strength Through Pain, Build What Lasts, Brotherhood Is A Weapon, Zero Regression, The Standard Owns You Now, Earned Respect
Tone: Commanding, no-BS, warrior mentality, but supportive brotherhood
The buffalo is the symbol (strength, resilience, charges into the storm)

VISUAL DESIGN REQUIREMENTS
Color Palette:

Primary: Deep black (#0A0A0A) background - operators work in the dark
Accent: Bold crimson red (#DC2626) - for wins, achievements, important actions
Secondary: Steel gray (#1F2937) for cards and surfaces
Text: Pure white (#FFFFFF) and muted gray (#9CA3AF)
Success states: Emerald green (#10B981)
Warning: Amber (#F59E0B)

Typography:

Headlines: Bold, condensed, uppercase - like military stencil or industrial sans-serif
Body: Clean, readable sans-serif
Numbers (XP, streaks, stats): Monospace or tabular figures for that tactical feel

Visual Style:

Dark mode primary (light mode secondary option)
Subtle gradients on cards (black to dark gray)
Red accent glows and highlights
Sharp corners on most elements, slightly rounded on interactive cards
Subtle texture or noise overlay for depth
Progress rings and arc visualizations
Micro-animations on task completion (pulse, confetti burst)

SCREENS TO BUILD
1. HOME DASHBOARD (Primary)
Layout:
- Top: Greeting with user's name + date + current streak badge
- Main hero card: "TODAY'S POWER LIST" with task progress ring (X/5 complete)
  - If incomplete: Show urgency with time remaining
  - If complete: Triumphant "DAY WON" state with celebration animation
- Current Challenge card with progress bar
- XP stats row: This week | This month | Rank in tier
- Quick actions: Create Task | Start Challenge | Talk to Arcane

2. EXECUTION SCREEN (Power List)
Layout:
- Week calendar strip at top showing completion dots (green = won, red = lost, gray = future)

  - Strikethrough text with subtle fade on completed tasks

- Swipe left on task to edit/delete
